Birth Control Sponge

It is a small, disk-shaped device made of soft plastic foam that covers the cervix and contains the spermicide nonoxynol-9 (N-9). The spermicide in the sponge prevents pregnancy by killing sperm, stopping them from reaching and fertilizing an egg.
