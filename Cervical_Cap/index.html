Cervical Cap / Diaphragm

It is a silicone cups that are used with a special gel or cream called spermicide (a substance that kills sperm). The diaphragm or cervical cap is inserted into the vagina and over the cervix to keep sperm from entering the uterus, meeting with and fertilizing an egg.
